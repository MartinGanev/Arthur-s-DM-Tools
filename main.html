DM Tools

Why keep a thousand pages of every possible player/npc interaction at hand? Why write about 10 pages worth of D&D encounters? Why draw that one scene from your last campaign where the bard charmed the dragon and the dragon decided to f*** him? Are you tired of all of that? Well then this is what you exactly need!

DM Tools is a project made by me, Arthur Deadmun. It's a simulation of various D&D encounters, not only with monsters, but with merchants, blacksmiths, priests, npc-s. It's been in development for 2 years already and its non-profit, but if you wish, you can support the team on patreon (link at the end).
